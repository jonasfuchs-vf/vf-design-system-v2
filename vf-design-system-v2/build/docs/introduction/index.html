Introduction

#Welcome to the Vodafone Design Experience

#Constant improvement through agile processes

Keeping in mind that web design has to be responsive, these design principles will make sure you always choose the right means in order to convey the same message on every screen.

The purpose of the Vodafone Digital Design is to establish a common web experience across Vodafone.de and the MeinVodafone App. Based on the information we have about our users and potential customers we constantly strive to improve the web experience by creating a consistent design as well as optimising customer and user experience journeys. Improving the performance on our digital platforms with regards to financial, strategic, and commercial driven KPIs also leads to commercial and strategic success.

At Vodafone Germany UX designers have to align a set of customer values, business needs, and design principles. This requires an exceptional dimension of creativity and flexibility throughout the creative process itself in order to achieve profitable solutions.
